Program Overview

This One-year Graduate Certificate program prepares students to work as multimedia designers and producers. The knowledge and experience gained are now standard in a wide variety of fields.

Graduates may find employment in dynamic media development, web design, server-side development, photography, videography, motion graphics, project management, UX design, graphic design and layout, online education. Other opportunities involve start-up businesses and a wide variety of alternate professional fields.

Faculty

Amel

Amel is a part time professor and freelance web developer/designer.
He has been teaching web scripting at Algonquin since Winter 2014. Amel graduated in 2009 from the Algonquin College with diploma in Interactive Multimedia (today Interactive Media Management).
From 2009 to 2014, he worked in the industry as a front-end developer. Amel also graduated from the University of Ljubljana (Slovenia) in 1997 with degree in industrial engineering. He worked as industrial engineer until 2004 when Amel and his family moved from Bosnia and Herzegovina to Canada.
